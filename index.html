ABOUT US

National Nursing College

Tongi, Gazipur, Bangladesh

The National Nursing College (NNC), situated in Tongi, Gazipur, is an institution recognized by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare (MHFW) and the Nursing and Midwifery Council (NMC). It is a health and educational academy affiliated with Dhaka University (DU). It is one of the best health and educational academies in Bangladesh. The National Nursing College's mission is to help build a health institute by training qualified nursing professionals. We anticipate that our graduates will make significant contributions to society on a global scale. Instead of an old-fashioned education system, we are offering the most advanced technological-based education to our students so that we can maintain the standard of our healthcare system. It also aids in alleviating the nation's unemployment crisis. READ MORE

OUR COURSES

Bachelor of Science in Nursing (Basic)

The purpose of a Bachelor of Science in Nursing program is to educate future nurses who have the knowledge, skills, and disposition to promote, maintain, and restore the health of individuals, families, and communities in response to both common and uncommon health issues and problems.

Learn More

Bachelor of Science in Nursing (Post Basic)

The purpose of a B.Sc. in Post Basic Nursing program is to provide higher education for future nurses. The course consists of two years and is designed for registered nurses who have completed the Diploma in Nursing and the Diploma in Midwifery, or Orthopedics Nursing or Psychiatric Nursing, or both.

Learn More

Diploma in Nursing Science and Midwifery

The institutional goal of Diploma in Nursing Science and Midwifery program is to produce competent nurses that will enable them for demonstrate the competency of using knowledge-based practice for provision of quality holistic client centered nursing care to meet the needs/expectations and to promote, maintain, and restore heath of individuals, families, and communities nationally and internationally.

Learn More

Diploma in Midwifery

The B.Sc. in Post Basic Nursing course consists of two years and designed for registered nurses who completed Diploma in Nursing and Diploma in Midwifery / Orthopedics Nursing / Psychiatric Nursing and who completed Diploma in Nursing Science and Midwifery course.

Learn More

Medical Assistance (MATS)

The overall goal of Bachelor of Science in Nursing degree is to produce competent professional nurses who will be able to demonstrate their knowledge, skills, and attitude to provide quality of nursing care in the aspects of the practical field to meet expected needs and expectations and to promote, maintain and restore health of individuals, families, and communities in common, simple and complex health problems/situations.
