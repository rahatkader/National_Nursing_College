ABOUT US

National Nursing College

Tongi, Gazipur, Bangladesh

National Nursing College is located at Sataish Chowrasta, Gazipura Bus Stand, Tongi. The NNC is located in a noise-free, secluded and pleasant environment.
There are qualified subject teachers and clinical instructors to accompany the students during the practice. Enam Nursing College is adjacent to Enam Medical College Hospital which has a sufficient number of outdoor and indoor patients at all times to facilitate medical and service training for nurses and they have the opportunity to receive hands-on training as skilled nurses.
It has its own well-equipped 650-seat auditorium with a state-of-the-art digital sound system and central air conditioning. There are also fire extinguishers and emergency rescue equipment on each floor. READ MORE

OUR COURSES

Bachelor of Science in Nursing (Basic)

The overall goal of Bachelor of Science in Nursing degree is to produce competent professional nurses who will be able to demonstrate their knowledge, skills, and attitude to provide quality of nursing care in the aspects of the practical field to meet expected needs and expectations and to promote, maintain and restore health of individuals, families, and communities in common, simple and complex health problems/situations.

Learn More

Bachelor of Science in Nursing (Post Basic)

The B.Sc. in Post Basic Nursing course consists of two years and designed for registered nurses who completed Diploma in Nursing and Diploma in Midwifery / Orthopedics Nursing / Psychiatric Nursing and who completed Diploma in Nursing Science and Midwifery course.
